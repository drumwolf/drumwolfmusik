Welcome to the Electric Circus Show!  This is the first of a regular series of webcasts bringing you random
sounds by random underground, alternative and indie music artists.

This particular playlist, "Noisy Pop and Poppy Noise," features shoegaze, dream-pop, noise pop, post-punk and psychedelic rock,
from the '90s to the present and combining the more legendary names with obscure artists that need more exposure.
